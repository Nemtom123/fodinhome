nanogallery2 is unique.





Highly customizable and eye-catching effects

nanogallery2 is fully configurable to suit your personal needs.
Create beautiful interactions and hover effects combinations to capture the attention of your visitors.
Natively touch enabled and responsive - unique user experience on all device types.

Focus on building your website

Just apply settings in the visual builder and see the result directly on screen. No coding. You can then generate ready to use HTML templates.

Designers and developers can of course do much more fine tuning by writing their own gallery in HTML. Full detailed documentation is provided.




Content source

Define each image individually or use the PHP add-on nanoPhotosProvider2 to stream your photos automatically from your own web server.
Flickr and Google Photos storage also natively supported.

Gallery customization is done regardless of your content source.

What's new since the popular nanoGALLERY?

Improved display algorithm for better user experience and performances.

New features include filter by keywords, shopping cart, social sharing, combinable and chainable hover effects, tools on thumbnails, more button, thumbnail display transitions, location on google maps, pinch zoom, thumbnail dominant colors...




Open Source

nanogallery2 is open source and hosted on Github. Everyone can look behind the scene and can contribute directly on Github. Pull Requests welcome!
